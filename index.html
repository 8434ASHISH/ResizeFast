How to use Resizefast

  1. Upload your photo: Click the upload box or drag and drop your image file.
  2. Choose a method: Select how you want to resize (by Pixel, Percentage, or target KB size).
  3. Adjust settings: Enter your desired width, height, or maximum file size.
  4. Preview & Download: Click "Resize & Preview" to see the result. If satisfied, click the download link.
  5. Convert to PDF: Need a document? Click "Convert to PDF" to save the image as a PDF file immediately.
